The problem

Chefs change. Kitchens slip. Owners stop caring. Most platforms show you an average of reviews from years ago and call it a score. Remark only shows you right now.

Problem 01

Scores that never expire

A restaurant coasts on great reviews from 3 years ago while quality quietly collapses. Remark's window means scores reflect today, not history.

Problem 02

Reviews from people who weren't there

Anyone can review anything on most platforms. Remark requires something that can't be faked: proof you were actually there, at that time, at that table.

Problem 03

Complaints that vanish into a void

Report something on other platforms and watch it disappear. On Remark, every complaint is human-reviewed and the resolution is posted publicly.
