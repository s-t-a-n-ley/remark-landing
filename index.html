The problem

That 4.5★ rating
is from 2021

Chefs change. Kitchens slip. Owners stop caring. Most platforms show you an average of reviews from years ago and call it a score. Remark only shows you right now.

Problem 01

Scores that never expire

A restaurant coasts on great reviews from 3 years ago while quality quietly collapses. Remark's 30-day window means scores reflect today, not history.

Problem 02

Reviews from people who weren't there

Anyone can review anything on most platforms. Remark requires GPS check-in and a timestamped photo at the time of the meal. No visit, no review.

Problem 03

Complaints that vanish into a void

Got food poisoning? Report it somewhere, it disappears. On Remark, every complaint is human-reviewed and the resolution is posted publicly.
